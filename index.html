Commitment to this community

Roy Sundstrom didn't just become a plumber — he was born into the trade. Spending 17 years learning alongside his father Bud, Roy earned every callus before ever earning his license. After seven years serving in the Army National Guard and graduating from Stockton University, he came home to Cape May and did the one thing that felt natural: he built something of his own.

Sundstrom Plumbing launched in 2021. When Roy isn't running pipe, you'll find him surfing at Headquarters Beach or chasing his kids — Maverick, Dawson, and Stella — around the yard with his wife. This is his town. These are his neighbors. That's the difference.
